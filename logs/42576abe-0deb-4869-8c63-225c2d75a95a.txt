1. Determined required syntax Verb-Object-Subject in Tizin.
2. Verb root for intense like (present): Maktay.
3. Direct object for oneself (accusative): mato.
4. Subject (apples, nominative): apple.
5. Combined as per structure: Maktay mato apple (translates to is pleasing to me: apples, i.e., I like apples).
